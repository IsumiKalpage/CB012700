Venues


Nuwara Eliya

Known as Sri Lanka's "Little England," Nuwara Eliya's cool climate and fertile soil make it ideal for growing vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and cabbage, amidst picturesque tea plantations.

Badulla

Badulla boasts rich agricultural lands where a variety of vegetables such as beans, cauliflower, and leeks thrive, contributing significantly to the region's economy.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura also serves as a hub for vegetable cultivation. Its warm climate supports the growth of tomatoes, onions, and green chilies in abundance.
